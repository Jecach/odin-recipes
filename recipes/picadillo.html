Picadillo

The picadillo is a traditional central american dish composed of a variety of boiled vegetables and meat.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Potatoes
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Prepare the Vegetables by cutting them in small cubes
  2. Start by adding water to a boiling pan and then add the cutted potatoes
  3. Add salt to the boiler and spices of your like
  4. After the potatoes/water mix gets some texture add ground meat
  5. Keep cokking until it dries out, lower the fire intensity if needed

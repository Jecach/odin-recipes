Gallo pinto

The Gallo Pinto is a traditional central american dish made from rice, beans and spices such as sweet pepper and lizano sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Beans
  • Salt
  • Sweet Pepper, Tomato, etc

Steps

  1. Add the beans along with any desired spices first into a pan.
  2. Add rice accordingly.
  3. Mix with Lizano Sauce and other spices.
  4. Serve.

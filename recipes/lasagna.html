Lasagna

The Lasagna is a traditional pasta dish eaten all over the world, its composed of several layers of pasta sheets with different ingredients in between.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Vegetables
  • Meat
  • Tomate Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Vegetables by cutting them in an appropiatte size.
  2. Cook the meat along the tomato sauce.
  3. With the pasta sheets as base start building layers of meat-sauce and vegetables. Use cheese as a glue between pasta and non pasta layer
  4. Once finished bake
